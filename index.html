About

TO BE WORLD CLASS TECH COMPANY

Gazellle Creek Technologies (PTY) LTD / Cooperations is a proudly South African company integrated in the technology and innovation industry located in Pretoria

Gazelle Creek Technologies (G.C.T) founded in 2020 by a group of four young men inspired by innovation and technology to solve life's contemporary day challenges, and to play a critical role in the (4.I.R) fourth industrial revolution space by Home Automation, Smart farming, Access Control systems, Web development / Mobile App remote controlling, Lighting systems, and Consultation.

Our driving purpose and motivation is to provide a world class superior quality and results to our customers and the world at large whilst conquering day to day challenges that will enhance growth for our company and clients.
