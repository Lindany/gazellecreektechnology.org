Smart Switch

This app uses raspberry pi to connect multiple camera's constantly monitoring the incidents, where the owner can view the place in real-time from any place around the world. The events are not recorded, up until the user captures / records or the system detects a human being. The camera can also automatically switch on the recording camera as soon as it detect a moving object in the approximity of 5 metres around. Theres also an option to notify the control room or request the emergency action reaction services, to respond to the incidents taking place... Sharing the link of the live stream.
